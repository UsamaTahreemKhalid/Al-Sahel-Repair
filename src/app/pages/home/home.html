Common Home Appliance Troubleshooting in the UAE

Why does my AC keep turning off and on (short-cycling)?

Short-cycling is often caused by a dirty air filter or an oversized unit. However, in Sharjah and Dubai, it's frequently due to a failing capacitor or a refrigerant leak. Constant cycling wastes energy and puts stress on the compressor.

How do I fix a refrigerator that is not cooling but the light is on?

Check if the condenser coils are dusty; they should be cleaned every six months. If the coils are clean, the issue might be a faulty evaporator fan or a defective start relay on the compressor.

Why is my washing machine shaking violently during the spin cycle?

This is usually caused by an unbalanced load or the machine not being level on the floor. Check the leveling legs and ensure you're not overloading the drum. If the shaking continues, the shock absorbers might be worn out.
