Proactive Troubleshooting & Home Appliance Care

Why is my AC blowing warm air in Sharjah?

If your AC is not cooling, first check if the thermostat is set to "Cool" and the temperature is lower than the room. Next, inspect your air filters; clogged filters restrict airflow and cause the evaporator coils to freeze. In the dusty UAE climate, we recommend cleaning filters every two weeks.

How do I fix a washing machine that won't drain?

A common cause for drainage issues is a clogged pump filter or a kinked drain hose. Check the small door at the bottom front of your machine to clear any coins or lint from the filter. If the issue persists, your drain pump might need professional replacement by our Dubai repair team.

Why is my refrigerator leaking water on the floor?

This usually happens due to a blocked defrost drain. Food particles or ice can clog the drain hose, causing water to overflow and leak. Try flushing the drain from the inside with warm water. If you see ice buildup on the back wall, your defrost heater or sensor may be failing.
